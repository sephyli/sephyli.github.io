01. About Me

I am a PhD student at Fudan University and Shanghai Innovation Institute, conducting research at OpenDriveLab under the supervision of Prof. Hongyang Li.

My research focuses on autonomous driving, lane detection, end-to-end AD systems, 3D reconstruction, and generative models. I'm passionate about developing intelligent systems that can perceive and understand the world in 3D.

Previously, I completed my Bachelor's and Master's degrees at Beihang University and gained valuable research experience at Sensetime and Megvii.
